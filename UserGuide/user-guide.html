How to Use
Engaging Reader

Upload a File

Upload any pdf document or picture onto Engaging Reader to instantly turn it into clear, easy-to-read text

  1. Go to engagingreader.com
  2. Click "Upload File"
  3. Select a file from your computer's file browser.
  4. It may take up to 60 seconds to convert a file. If it has been longer than a minute, reload the site and try uploading again.
  5. When the text appears, use Play or the spacebar to start listening.
Translation is available after upload for supported image and PDF files.

Listen to a Text

The play bar lets you start listening, pause, change the reading speed, and follow the word that is being read aloud.

ControlWhat it does
Play
Starts reading the text aloud.
Pause
Stops the reading at any time.
1x Reading Speed
Shows the current reading speed. Click it to choose another speed like .5x, 1x, or 2x.
word Highlight
The yellow highlight shows the word being read right now.

Define Words

Click on words for context-specific definitions that make sense

  1. Click a word to define it.
  2. Word definitions are clear, simple, and explain the meaning in the context of the sentence.
  3. Definitions are narrated, and words are highlighted just like the main text.
  4. Click the X to return to the main text. The narration will start automatically from the word you defined.

Keyboard Shortcuts

A keyboard shortcut is a quick way to do something on a computer by pressing keys on your keyboard at the same time.

ShortcutAction
Left / RightMove from word to word.
Up / DownMove from paragraph to paragraph.
EnterDefine the selected word.
SpacePlay or pause the narration.
EscLeave the definition and return to the main text.

Using your Phone

You can access Engaging Reader easily on your mobile device at engagingreader.com

  1. Press Upload File.
  2. Select "Photo Library" to use a photo from your camera roll, or select "Take Photo" to capture a new photo.
  3. Tap a word to define it.
  4. Playback and narration work just like on the computer.
